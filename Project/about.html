About Us

Luxury Autos is a leading car dealership and solutions provider for the automotive industry that connects car shoppers with sellers. Launched in 2004 and headquartered in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. the Company empowers shoppers with the data, resources, and digital tools needed to make informed buying decisions and seamlessly connect with automotive retailers. In a rapidly changing market, Luxury Autos enables dealerships and OEMs with innovative technical solutions and data-driven intelligence to better reach and influence ready-to-buy shoppers, increase inventory turn and gain market share.
